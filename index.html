About me

My name is Meedaxa and I am a web developer based in Birmingham, UK. I am 25 years old and originally from Ethiopia. I love playing football and reading. I have been interested in programming since my early teens but I never got round to learning how to code professionally until recently. I hope to create a fruitful career out of coding, and I believe I am on my way to achieving that.

I am what they call a full-stack developer which means I am able to do both front-end and back-end development. I am well-versed in the three main languages that constitute web development. These are as follows:

  • HTML-The content of the web application
  • CSS- The styling of the web application
  • Javascript-Used to add dynamics and facilitate user interaction with the web application
My favourite language out of the three is javascript because of its diverse functions. Most web applications nowadays are interactive and dynamic and javascript is the reason for that. Although I wouldn't call myself a bootstrap developer, I am also well-versed in utilising the bootstrap framework to create responsive web applications.
